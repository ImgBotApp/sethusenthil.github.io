info What is this?

Blank Folders is an experimental project which allows you to make empty folders that completely renders a blank name (without a "?" while opening a folder) utilizing a unicode exploit.

help Instructions

Click on the button to copy the text, then navigate to your home screen. While renaming your folder, paste from your clipboard.
